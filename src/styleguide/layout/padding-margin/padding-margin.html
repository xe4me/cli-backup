AMP's css framework caters for a variety of helper classes.

Below is a list of helper classes and simple demos of their use

Padding

In order to use padding effectively, it's important to understand whats available for use.

p0 = no padding

pt = padding top

pb = padding bottom

pl = padding left

pr = padding right

Padding combinations

pv = padding vertical

pv = padding horizontal

Sizing

The amount of padding is calculated dynamically. The padding will then either be reduced or increased based on the suffix you give your class.

Note: It is not recommend to have any other permutations of these classes, such as adding in your own width for the padding itself as this will create inconsistencies in your layout.

pl- = padding left 50%

pl-- = padding left 25%
